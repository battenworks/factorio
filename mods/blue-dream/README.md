# blue-dream

Flying in a Blue Dream

That's the title to an old Joe Satriani album I once had.
It was the first album where we heard him sing.

This is the replacement for my old `train-city` mod that no longer works in Factorio 2.x.
I'm figuring out how to make it function correctly in the new Factorio version, first.
The mod will load in 2.x, but the dashboard doesn't display all the tabs I had.
Nor does it display item widgets the way it should.
So, I need to get the dashboard working correctly.
Then, I'll try to take advantage of the new behavior with trains.

Funny thing about the new train groups in 2.x.
They are needed in the game for the same reason I created `train-city`.
My motivation for the mod was that I was spending too much time configuring each new train I crafted and deployed.
I had already devised a system with stops and locomotives that are grouped to service a particular item type.
I did it by giving stops and locomotives consistent, predictable, names that effectively created groups.
Now that groups are a first-class citizen in the game, I'm interested in leveraging them.

Another thing I was going to add to `train-city` was the idea of "personas".
Think, "I'm building city blocks", or "I'm terraforming and extending the network".
You choose your persona and your inventory is automatically converted to the needs of the persona.
Now, with groups in your inventory, I might be able to leverage that to accomplish the persona behavior.
